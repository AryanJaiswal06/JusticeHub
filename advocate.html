Analytics


The analytics module provides a comprehensive overview of advocate performance across various judicial levels. Based on the current data, advocates practicing in the Supreme Court demonstrate the highest average success rate of approximately 96%, reflecting strong case preparation, strategic litigation skills, and extensive courtroom experience.


High Court advocates maintain an impressive performance index, with consistent outcomes in constitutional, civil, and criminal matters. District and Civil Court practitioners show steady growth trends, highlighting increasing efficiency and specialization in their respective domains. The system evaluates metrics such as case resolution rate, client satisfaction, average case duration, and overall win percentage to generate these insights.


These analytics assist administrators and stakeholders in identifying top-performing advocates, optimizing case allocation, and enhancing transparency within the legal ecosystem. Continuous performance monitoring ensures accountability and promotes a data-driven approach to justice delivery.
